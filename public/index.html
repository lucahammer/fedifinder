Fedifinder

Extract the fediverse handles of your Twitter followings and import them on Mastodon to follow them all at once.

Find fediverse handles of your followings

Fedifinder looks for @user@host.tld, user@host.tld and host.tld/@user in the name, description, location and url field.
Because of Twitter API rate limiting, only the most recent 3000 followings are looked at. If you get a "Rate limit exceeded" warning, wait about 15 minutes and try again.

Made by @Luca@vis.social. Source code in the fish menu (top right) and on Github. Discuss the tool in the fediverse.
